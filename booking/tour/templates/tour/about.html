About Sajilo Yatra

Crafting unforgettable journeys through Nepal's breathtaking landscapes

Our Story

Sajilo Yatra was founded with a simple mission: to make Nepal's incredible natural and cultural treasures accessible to travelers from all around the world. "Sajilo" means "easy" in Nepali, and that's exactly what we do - we make booking your dream Nepal adventure easy and hassle-free.

With over a decade of experience in the tourism industry, our team of passionate travel experts has crafted carefully curated tour packages that showcase the best of what Nepal has to offer. From the majestic Himalayan peaks to the spiritual temples of Kathmandu, from the serene lakes of Pokhara to the remote beauty of Mustang, we've got it all covered.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for thousands of travelers. We believe that every journey should be memorable, and we work tirelessly to make that happen.
